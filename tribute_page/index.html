Here's a time line of Disney's Life:

  • 1901 - Born in Chicago
  • 1906 - The Disney family moves to Marceline, Missouri. This is where Walt became interested in drawing.
  • 1911 - The Disney family moves to Kansas City, Missouri. Here Walt began taking classes at the Kansas City Art Institute.
  • 1917 - The Disney family moves back to Chicago. Walt becomes the cartoonist for his high school newspaper.
  • 1918 - After not being able to join the army because he was too young, Walt joins the Red Cross as an ambulance driver and is sent to France.
  • 1921 - Walt starts his own business: Laugh-O-Grams Studios.
  • 1923 - Laugh-O-Grams Studios goes bankrupt. Walt signs a contract for his Alice Comedies shorts. Walt and his brother Roy start the Disney Brothers Studio in Los Angeles.
  • 1925 - Walt marries Lillian Bounds.
  • 1928
    • Walt loses the rights to Oswald the Lucky Rabbit and most of his staff leaves to work for a competitor.
    • Mickey (originally dubbed Mortimer) is created in response to the loss of Oswald.
  • 1931 - After trouble with the business Walt has a nervous breakdown. He and Lillian take an extended vacation to Cuba to recover.
  • 1932 - Walt wins an Academy Award for best Short Subject (Cartoon) for Flowers and Trees and an honorary award for the creation of now internationally popular Mickey Mouse.
  • 1934 - Tired with creating only shorts, Walt begins the four year long production of Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs.
  • 1939 - Snow White becomes the most successful sound film to date and Walt wins another academy award for it. One full sized awards and seven dwarf sized awards.
  • 1954 - Construction begins on Disneyland in Anaheim, California.
  • 1955 - Disneyland opens during a live broadcast on ABC. More than 70 million viewers watch the opening ceremony.
  • 1964 - Walt develops four attractrion for the World's Fair:
    • It's a Small World
    • Great Moments with Mr. Lincoln
    • The Carousel of Progress
    • Ford's Magic Skyway
  • 1965 - Walt announces his plans to open Disney World just southwest of Orlando, Florida.
  • 1966 - After a lifetime of heavy smoking, Walt is diagnosed with lung cancer and died less than a month later..
  • 1971 - In honor of Walt, Disney World is renamed Walt Disney World and opens on October 1.
